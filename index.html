Welcome to SillyCon ( OWASP Kolkata Chapter )

The Open Web Application Security Project (OWASP) is a 501(c)(3) worldwide not-for-profit charitable organization focused on improving the security of software. Our mission is to make software security visible, so that individuals and organizations worldwide can make informed decisions about true software security risks.

What we provide

IT Training

We provide free IT Trainning.Most of the training are possible because of the contributors who came forward to teach and share knowledge without any demand for expenses.

Security Conferences

We conduct open security conferences.Most of the conferences are possible because of the contributors who came forward to teach and share knowledge without any demand for expenses

Internships

SillyCon is a place to showcase talent.Become a contributor, share your knowledge with others and in return we will help you in finding a right internship .

Our Team

We have industry experts in our group in the fields of Information Security, Open Source, Cloud, DevOps etc who have joined hands to develop a better IT ecosystem, where young and eager minds of today can be nurtured for developing them into the innovator of tomorrow.Contact us if you would like to be a part of this team
